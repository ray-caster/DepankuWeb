When editing your organization listing, please ensure:

Organization Editing Help
Editing Your Organization

Welcome to Depanku's organization editing form. This guide will help you keep your listing current and effective.

Best Practices for Updates
  • Keep Information Current: Update deadlines, requirements, and contact information regularly
  • Remove Expired Positions: Delete positions that are no longer accepting applications
  • Add New Opportunities: Include any new internships, research positions, or job openings
  • Update Descriptions: Revise organization descriptions to reflect current focus and achievements
What to Update
  • Application Deadlines: Remove expired deadlines and add new ones
  • Position Requirements: Update skills and qualifications as needed
  • Contact Information: Ensure email addresses and websites are still valid
  • Organization Details: Update mission, values, or recent achievements
Review Process

All edits to your organization listing are reviewed by our team within 24-48 hours. We check for:

  • Accuracy and relevance of updated information
  • Removal of expired or outdated content
  • Appropriate additions of new opportunities
  • Overall quality and usefulness of the listing
Tips for Effective Updates
  • Make changes regularly to keep your listing fresh
  • Respond promptly to student inquiries about updated positions
  • Use clear, specific language in position descriptions
  • Include application deadlines prominently
